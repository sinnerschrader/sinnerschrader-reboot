The North German agency for digital commerce, with its custom-fit content concept and innovative design ideas, was most convincing. In addition, SinnerSchrader Commerce can fall back on extensive experience in the maintenance business for large German resellers.</p><p dir="ltr">With its ten-strong core team SinnerSchrader Commerce implements around 45 digital campaigns for all HSE24 topic areas with its facilities in Hamburg and Prague. These include the complete production chain of the campaign concepts for the content production and adaption, as well as the text production and quality assurance. In addition, the agency assumes the continued strategy consulting and further development of the management shop.
